# Customer Retention Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer churn behavior in a European bank dataset using Excel, Python, Pandas, Plotly, and Streamlit.

The objective is to identify customer retention patterns, understand factors contributing to churn, and provide data-driven business recommendations through an interactive dashboard.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze customer churn patterns
- Identify factors influencing customer retention
- Visualize customer demographics and behavior
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making
- Generate actionable retention strategies

---

## Tools & Technologies

- Microsoft Excel
- Python
- Pandas
- Plotly
- Streamlit
- GitHub

---

## Key Findings

- Female customers showed higher churn rates.
- Germany recorded the highest churn rate among all regions.
- Inactive customers were more likely to leave the bank.
- Customers with 3–4 products showed increased churn probability.
- Churned customers maintained higher average account balances.

---

## Business Recommendations

### 1. Customer Retention Programs
Develop targeted retention campaigns for customer segments showing high churn rates.

### 2. Region-Specific Strategies
Implement specialized customer engagement initiatives for high-risk regions, especially Germany.

### 3. Improve Customer Engagement
Increase communication and personalized offers for inactive customers.

### 4. Product Optimization
Promote suitable banking products and services to strengthen customer relationships.

### 5. Early Churn Detection
Use predictive indicators to identify at-risk customers before they leave.

### 6. High-Value Customer Monitoring
Closely monitor customers with high account balances to reduce revenue loss.
